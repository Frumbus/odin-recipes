Tie Fighter Snack

Ingredients:

  • 2 squares graham crackers
  • 1 large marshmallow
  • 1/2 tsp peanut butter

Instructions:

  1. Use the peanut butter to "glue" the marshamallow in between the graham crackers. Fly your TIE fighter around for a bit and you can add your own sound effects. Enjoy!
